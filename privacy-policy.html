Privacy Policy for WA Group Contacts Extractor

Effective Date: [Insert Date]

Introduction

Thank you for using the WA Group Contacts Extractor. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy outlines how we handle any data while you use the extension.

Information Collection

The WA Group Contacts Extractor extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All data, including the contact information extracted from WhatsApp groups, remains local to your device. The extension processes the information directly on your browser and exports it to a CSV file locally.

Data Usage

The extension's sole purpose is to extract contacts from WhatsApp groups and export them into a CSV file. The data extracted is not shared, transmitted, or sent to any third-party services or servers.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, we do not store or have access to any of your personal data. All extracted information is processed locally on your device, ensuring that you remain in full control of your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if any significant changes are made to the extension. If we make changes, we will post the updated policy on this page, so please review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your privacy while using the extension, do not contact us..
